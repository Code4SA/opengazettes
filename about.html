Open Gazettes is the largest freely available online collection of gazettes in South Africa.

OpenGazettes.org.za is a collaboration between a number of partners to make South African Government Gazettes freely available to anyone. Gazettes are a critical source of information and record of South African history. They should be easy to find and use, and freely available to everyone.

Join the liberation!

Contact us

If you would like to get in touch or contribute gazettes, you can contact us on gazettes@code4sa.org or go to opengazettes.org.za/join and complete the #GazetteLiberation form.

API access to the gazettes

There are a few ways of getting the gazettes programatically. If you need help please email gazettes@code4sa.org.

This is an atom feed of the 100 most recently added gazettes. This is useful to put into your feed reader to find out when we add new gazettes.

  • http://code4sa-gazettes.s3.amazonaws.com/archive/recently-added.atom

All gazettes atom feed

This is an atom feed of ALL gazettes. Take care, this currently over 10MB big.

  • http://code4sa-gazettes.s3.amazonaws.com/archive/all.atom

JSON index

This is an index of the metadata of all gazettes in our collection is available in the JSON lines format, one gazette per line.

Open Gazettes is the largest freely available online collection of Gazettes in South Africa.

OpenGazettes.org.za is a collaboration between a number of partners to make South African Government Gazettes freely available to anyone. Gazettes are a critical source of information and record of South African history. They should be easy to find and user, and freely available to everyone.

API Access to the Gazettes

There are a few ways of getting the gazettes programatically. If you need help please email gazettes@code4sa.org.

Recently Added Gazettes Atom Feed

An atom feed of the 100 most recently added gazettes. This is useful to put into your feed reader to find out when we add new gazettes.

  • http://code4sa-gazettes.s3.amazonaws.com/archive/recently-added.atom

All Gazettes Gazettes Atom Feed

An atom feed of ALL gazettes. Take care, this currently over 10MB big.

  • http://code4sa-gazettes.s3.amazonaws.com/archive/all.atom

JSON Index

An index of the metadata of all gazettes in our collection is available in the JSON lines format, one gazette per line.

  • http://code4sa-gazettes.s3.amazonaws.com/archive/index/latest.jsonlines
